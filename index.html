About Based Undeads

Based Undeads rise from the ashes on Base, born in the forge of blockchain immortality. Each of the 6,666 souls carries its own legend, forged in fire, bound by chaos, and eternally etched on-chain. These aren't just NFTs—they're the restless spirits of a digital graveyard, algorithmically summoned from the void.

From skeletal warriors to phantom wanderers, each Undead tells a story written in pixels and pain. The horde grows stronger. The Undead never die—they evolve, they persist, they become.

Which spirit calls to you? Join the revolution. Claim your Undead. Rise with the horde.

A Creator's Journey: This collection marks my first venture into pixel art—a leap from code into canvas, from developer to digital necromancer. Every sprite, every skull, every shadow was learned stroke by stroke. What started as experimentation became obsession. What began as pixels became souls.
